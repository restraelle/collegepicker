About The Project

As students in an engineering school, we know the standards and skills change every decade. With such a dynamic learning regimen, choosing or switching universities can be a hassle which usually has to do with a lot of research, a lot of browser tabs, head scratching, and overall, non enjoyment.

We decided this would be a good testing candidate for our Database Project: to reduce the headache that comes from trying to research multiple universities by having all of the information in one place. We referred to the National Center for Education Statistics for accurate information for our testing model.
